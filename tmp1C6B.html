Club Meetings


Note: in an attempt to avoid clashes of microscope club meeting dates, we are adding other club meeting information as we find out details. If any one wishes to add further information please e-mail Mike Samworth.


  • A new monthly microscope meeting in Wiltshire. details.


  • The Reading Convention, St. Peters Church Hall, Reading.Saturday March 9th 2019. Lectures, displays, sales. Please contact Mike Woof.


  • Midlands Members Meeting, Haling Dene Centre, Penkridge Staffs (ST19 5DT) on March 30th 2019. Lectures, displays, sales. Email Mike Woof.


  • The next PMS AGM will be on the 11th May 2019 at the Turville Memorial Hall in Husbands Bosworth (LE17 6JX). Again, the date has been chosen to avoid both the bank holiday and Dale Fort Plankton weekend. The hall will be open from 9:30 to 16:30, with the AGM at 12:00. If you require mains power for your equipment, please bring any extension reels and distribution extensions you may have, as power points are scarce.
    As before, mains equipment operated in the hall has to be PAT tested (testing can be carried out on the day at no charge).
    Please contact Mick Chaplin if you are willing to give a presentation. Note that it is not necessary to book tables in advance.
