Springtail of the Month - 2
 Continuation sheet 3

No sensory complex between Antenna segments 3/4; just a protrusible sac which becomes obvious in flattened head mounts. Pseudocelli absent.


Mandibles with strong molar plate, easily seen in cleared specimens.
