Springtail of the Month - 4
 Continuation sheet 1

Head Details:


On each side of the head are 2 "eyes" in separate pigment spots, the single ocelli in each are not visible at this magnification. Elongate post antennal organs can distort with flattening. Note regular rows of setae at posterior margins of the head, and Th2 to Abd3.
