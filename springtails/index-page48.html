Springtail of the Month - 5
 Continuation sheet 1


The remaining illustrations were made from specimens collected in October 2001  from the woodland bordering the E. shore of Lake Coniston in Cumbria. The specimens were photographed either entire in 70% isopropanol or mounted in Euparal or PVAL.  





   


Look back at Springtail of the Month number 1 (Tomocerus minor) and check the similarities: Long antennae, segments 3 & 4 flexible and annulated – rings of fine setae. Th 1 reduced dorsally; Abd4 less than 2.5x  length of Abd3. Head, body, legs, antennae and furca covered with dark scales; these are often detached in fresh or preserved specimens exposing the golden/brown cuticle beneath.
