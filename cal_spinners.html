PMS Digital Imaging Group

Spinners of Garden Spider (Araneus diadematus).




Silk is visible coming from the spinners.

Processed in Auto-Montage from ten 2048*1536 images taken on a Nikon Coolpix 995, through a Skance GSX zoom stereo microscope (made under licence from Zeiss Jeneval).

Lighting was by a home-constructed twin fibre-optic lamp with adjustable mirrors attached, to achieve a high illumination angle.

The spider was placed on a glass slide with a blue background some way beneath it, in order to render the background out of focus.

The imperfections in the background lighting could be said to add an artistic element (I like it anyway).
