PMS Digital Imaging Group

Garden Spider (Araneus diadematus) - Dorsal View.




Processed in Auto-Montage from 18 (2048*1536) images taken on a Nikon Coolpix 995, mounted on a copy stand. The camera was used in macro mode (closest focus distance 2 cm).

Lighting was by a home-constructed twin fibre-optic lamp with adjustable mirrors attached (to achieve a high illumination angle).

The specimen was placed on a glass slide and the background provided by a piece of coloured paper placed below the slide.

Note the silk thread visible between the 2 back legs.

Note: There was a problem in processing this series of images, as there was a noticeable change in the size of the spider image over the series and Auto-Montage could not cope. This was due to the low ratio between the spider distance to the camera lens and the thickness of the spider body. To enable Auto-Montage to cope, each image was individually resized in Paint Shop Pro.
