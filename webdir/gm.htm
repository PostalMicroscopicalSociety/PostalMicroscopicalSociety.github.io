Postal Microscopical Society Web Directory - General Microscopy
 Web Directory
Light Microscopy
A general site explaining the fundamentals and techniques of light microscopy. Designed for the use of students, hence includes exercises in examination of samples.
Optical Microscopy Primer
An excellently presented and designed site covering a wide range of microscopy topics including Confocal Microscopy, Fluorescence, Photomicrography and Digital Imaging. Time spent on this site will not be wasted!

Microscopy
Embracing lots of microscope and preparation techniques visitors to this site will be rewarded with a clear, concise text explaining the fundamentals of microscopy and slide preparation. There is also a recommended literature section.

Microscopy ListServer
The registration site for the Microscopy ListServer. This discussion forum is an ideal medium for asking those questions for which you have been unable to find an answer or even to get a simpler explanation of a problem.

Microscopy Online
An online forum for microscopists. The site includes an equipment exchange section which might yield that elusive accessory. Also included a Vendor’s Showcase and Buyer’s Guide.

Virtual Library - Microscopy
This quite useful site maintains a large list of sites related to Microscopy. Using a webcrawler the links are periodically (it says) updated and verified. However, the last time this happened appears to be November 2000 so some links may be redundant.

Microscopy Vendor Information
A site whose main function appears to be the provision of links to vendor sites. Having said that there is a section decoted to a techincal glossary and also one for School Microscopy.

Microscopy UK
The premier British microscopy website for all levels of microscope enthusiast. The deceptively plain front page fronts a whole host of sections and archives built up over a number of years. Regular contributors provide continuity and the associated online shop is worth a visit.
