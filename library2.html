PMS Library List


The Library facility is for the use of members only. The Society pays postage out and the member pays postage back. This list has been brought up to date (January 2001). The figures at the end of most entries represent the approximate weight of the book, in grammes; this allows members to calculate the required postage, however, not all books have been weighed. To loan a book please use the e-mail link to the librarian. A quick way to enter the book information is to "copy and paste" from the library list into the e-mail request.


Comments on books (or videos) borrowed can be of assistance to other members. To contribute comments please e-mail the webmaster.
