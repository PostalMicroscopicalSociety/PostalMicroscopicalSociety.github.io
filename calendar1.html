Syncroscopy 2005 Calendar Competition
by Mick Chaplin

Syncroscopy, the company which produces Auto-Montage, asked for Auto-Montage users to send in images. From the entries, 12 would be selected for their 2005 calendar. There was also a prize of an I-Pod for the best overall image. Thinking nothing ventured, etc. I entered 7 images. To my surprise 3 of the images were accepted for the calendar for the months of February, May and August (although not selected for best overall). I was even more surprised when I received my copy of the calendar to discover which organisations the other successful entrants came from:
January; School of Earth and Environmental Sciences, University of Adelaide.
March: St Barts,etc, University of London.
April: Havard University.
June and September: Tallinn University, Estonia.
July: Natural Resources, Canada.
October: California Academy of Sciences.
November: Academy of Natural Sciences, Philadelphia.
December: Natural History Museum, London.

It does show that in some areas enthusiastic amateurs can compete with professionals.
