PMS Digital Imaging Group

Hoverfly Head (Syrphus ribesii)





Processed in Auto-Montage from 59 (2048*1536) images taken on a Nikon Coolpix 995, through a Gillett and Sibert Conference microscope fitted with Russian 3.5 X 0.1 NA objective.

Lighting was by a home-constructed twin fibre-optic lamp with adjustable mirrors attached (to achieve a high illumination angle).

The specimen was placed on a glass slide and the background provided by a piece of coloured paper in the sub-stage condenser tray.

Note: This series of images could not be processed in one stage due to generation of a runtime error in Auto-Montage, probably caused by too many high resolution images. The series was split into two and each processed separately. The two results were then montaged together. An alternative approach would have been to re-photograph the images at, say, 1600*1200, or to re-size the originals.

The image suffers from having the specimen and background of similar colour, a pale blue background would have been better.
