PMS Digital Imaging Group

House Fly Head (Musca domestica)




This image also appears on the main digital imaging group page.

Processed in Auto-Montage from 46 1600*1200 images taken on a Nikon Coolpix 950, through a Gillett and Sibert Conference microscope fitted with Russian 3.5 X 0.1 NA objective. Lighting was by a home-constructed twin fibre-optic lamp with adjustable mirrors attached (to achieve a high illumination angle).

The specimen was glued to a bent pin which is visible but out of focus.
