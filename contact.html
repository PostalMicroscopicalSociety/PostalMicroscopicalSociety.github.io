Contacting Us


This section lists the PMS officers with e-mail addresses:


Secretary  Mike Samworth. Please address to the secretary any PMS queries or comments, not specifically covered by other committee members, including new membership. Please note that we now able to accept applications for on-circuit membership. Membership subscriptions are given in the 'What is the PMS?' section. . If you wish to join then please obtain a membership application form by e-mail from the secretary giving your address (obviously) .
